The Problem

Each year, around 45,000 people commit suicide within the United States. For each life taken, 25 more Americans attempt to kill themselves. Suicide is a major problem within our country, but it is not taken as seriously as possible. During this day and time, a majority of the younger generations avoid talking on the phone as much as possible and feel more comfortable instant messaging. These problems and more will be addressed through MOM.

Our Mission

Means of Omitting Melancholy, or MOM for short, is focused on helping anyone who may be suffering from depression and/or suicidal tendencies through the use of 24/7 online instant messaging between QPR certified volunteers and the victim. It is our goal to be there for anyone who may need this service, but can either not afford it or would rather instant message than talk on the phone.
