Thank you for choosing to create an account! Please choose whether you would like a professional or regular account. A description of each can be found below.

  • Professional Account

This account option is for anyone who currently is, or wants to be, certified in suicide prevention and become a volunteer counselor for this business. This would entail having the necessary QPR certification in suicide prevention, being available for the majority of the day, and understanding the importance of being there for the victim you will be messaging with. This is such an important responsibility and will need to be taken seriously!

  • Regular Account

If you have been impacted by suicide or depression at some point in your life, it is important for you to get the help that you need. By creating a regular account, you will be able to have completely free, 24/7 access to counselors who can help you when you need it most.
